The World of Somos.


This last unit seeks to explore and unravel the world of Somos. To build it, we made use of the “Double Screen” or Second Screen, which is a new way of viewing and enjoying the audiovisual elements offered by digital platforms. On a second screen, which may well be a mobile device, the viewer can search the Internet for additional information on topics and events depicted in the series, and thus learn about and document the story and the characters.

To do this, we outlined eight themes or case studies that illustrate the world of Somos. which allow us to examine the characters, peer into their daily life and culture, as well as explore the complexity of the region. The themes are:


  • 1. Culture and territory
  • 2. Northeastern Mexico’s architecture
  • 3. Cattle ranching culture - including northern Mexican barbecues or cookouts
  • 4. Drinks from northern Mexico
  • 5. Signs and local graphic art
  • 6. Norteño Music
  • 7. Quinceañeras
  • 8. Unique fashion of the border area


    • These topics discussed in the series allow us to humanize the characters, know their context and examine their daily lives. At the same time, they enable us to approach the unique culture of a territory like Coahuila, with specific social problems, such as drug trafficking and its proximity to the United States. With short clips taken from the production and multiple Internet resources, the experience is complemented to generate new bits of information.

    By exploring each of these topics, we not only recognize the characters and their way of life and coexistence, but we also access their community and their way of meeting and recognizing each other, both in private and in public space.
