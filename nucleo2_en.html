From Reality to Fiction


One of the greatest challenges of this series was to develop a fictional plot from a tragic story. To do so, the first thing was to take up the journalistic text of Ginger Thompson, understand it and rework it with an amazing group of writers: James Schamus, Fernanda Melchor and Monika Revillla. The next step was to bring together professionals who would bring the characters and the story to life, and to create the settings and environments where the storytelling takes place. To this end, in-depth research was carried out in the city of Allende regarding its location, customs, geography, topography and its problems. Once the territory was better understood, the creators of the series transformed reality into fiction. The quality of the images, the veracity in the creation of the story and the impeccable aesthetics in the six episodes demonstrate that a raw and painful moment can be presented to the public in a digestible yet forceful way


The series was filmed in Durango. For the re-creation of spaces and environments, the production took images from Thompson's text as a starting point. They then reviewed the work of prominent professional photographers such as Maya Goded, Dominic Bracco, Ruth Prieto, Alejandro Cartagena, Stephen Ferry and Joana Toro, who have all captured rural life in northern Mexico in their work. This all assisted in portraying life in Mexico near the border with the United States, with its poverty, religious devotion, drug trafficking, violence, customs and daily habits.


This unit consists of images by cinematographer Ignacio Prieto Palacios, and researchers and technicians who with curiosity chronicled the scenes, backstage interactions and moments that ultimately brought this fiction to life. The production documented each of the details and settings of the series, doing so with professional cameras and telephones with the intention of capturing both the landscapes and buildings, as well as the preparations, the anguishing and stressful moments, or the festive spirit after capturing a flawless shot.

The intention of this section is for the viewer to delve into the construction of the fictional universe involved in a production of this magnitude.
